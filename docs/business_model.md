# Business Model

## Subscription Model

Small stores, restaurants, and warehouses can pay monthly for waste prediction dashboards, expiry alerts, and prevention recommendations.

Suggested tiers:

- Starter: one location, manual inventory upload
- Growth: multiple locations, API access, CSV import/export
- Pro: POS integrations, advanced analytics, team workflows

## Restaurant and Store SaaS

Restaurants and grocery stores benefit from reduced spoilage, better discount timing, and donation workflows. Pricing can be based on number of locations, inventory volume, or estimated savings.

## Factory Enterprise Model

Factories can use overproduction prevention to reduce raw material waste, excess packaging, and finished-goods expiry. Enterprise pricing can include custom integrations, forecasting model tuning, and compliance reports.

## NGO and Government Partnerships

NGOs, food banks, shelters, and local governments can use aggregated surplus signals to plan pickups and reduce hunger. Public-sector partnerships can fund regional food waste reduction dashboards.

## Revenue Streams

- SaaS subscriptions
- Enterprise implementation fees
- API usage pricing
- Analytics reports for ESG and compliance
- Government and NGO coordination contracts
- Integration marketplace for POS, ERP, and delivery platforms
